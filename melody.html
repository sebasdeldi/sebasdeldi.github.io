MelodyGenie


I have always been a music lover but I constantly faced the problem of finding new artists that i might like, so in order to solve that issue for myself, I came up with this idea. MelodyGenie takes an artist that you like, and based

on that, it gives you a list of artists that are similar to it based on Spotify's database, you can see the amount of followers and popularity of each artist, and when an artist card is clicked you can see the most popular records, listen to a preview of each song, find it on spotify or make a google search for it's free version download.
