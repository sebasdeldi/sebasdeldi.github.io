TuCancha


TuCancha is a Colombian startup that connects amateur soccer players with soccer courts by giving them the possibility to rent the fields via mobile app and providing venues with a complete management system which includes

statistics about the rent perfomance during a day, a month and a year, aswell as all the booking information. At TuCancha I made part of the development team and my job was to develop a hybrid android client and contribute to the statistic charting system.
